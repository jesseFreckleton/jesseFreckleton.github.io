About

Since a very young age I’ve shown a passion for technology and known I’ve wanted to work in a field involving some type of tech. As a young kid I remember pouring over the Scholastic News articles about new inventions such as robots and trying to soak up all the information. When I was in junior high my family got a computer for us kids, I would spend hours on there. Looking at all the different options in the computer and wanting to figure out how it all worked. That passion continued to grow as I became an adult and started spending my free time teaching myself to code. While teaching myself to code I worked as a manager at Wendy’s which taught me many things including leadership and solving problem skills.

More recently however, I’ve been given the opportunity to start studying at College of Western Idaho (CWI) to get my Associates Degree in Software Development. In my first semester, as a student, I completed all the generals required by CWI in order to start the courses for my degree. Currently I’m about to finish my first semester as a software development student. During this time, I have learned how to become a better problem solver as well as how to start developing my professional persona.
